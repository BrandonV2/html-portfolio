The Best Movies According to Brandon

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime movie from Studio Ghibli. I love the animations and plot.

The Wolf of Wall Street

I love this movie because of it's plot twists and story revolving around the stock market.

Harry Potter

I love the Harry Potter movie series because of it's one of a kind story and characters. I love wizards and sorcery, so the movie is perfect for me.
